About Apple Inc.

Apple Inc. is a global technology leader renowned for its innovative products and services that have transformed the way people live, work, and communicate. Founded in 1976 by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne, Apple has consistently pushed the boundaries of design and functionality, creating iconic devices such as the iPhone, iPad, Mac computers, Apple Watch, and AirPods. The company's commitment to excellence extends beyond hardware to include software ecosystems like iOS, macOS, watchOS, and services such as the App Store, iCloud, Apple Music, and Apple TV+. With a focus on user experience, privacy, and sustainability, Apple continues to shape the future of technology while maintaining a strong emphasis on environmental responsibility and social impact.
